"First Dress"

“The swirling tulle merges with bold painted elements, a fusion of memories and aspirations.”

This piece marks my first attempt at fashion design, not just sketching ideas, but bringing them to life. As a child, I once dreamed of becoming a fashion designer. During the COVID-19 lockdown in Shanghai, I turned that childhood vision into reality. This dress became more than just fabric and stitches, it was my way of creating something meaningful in uncertain times. A personal milestone, a gift to my 17-year-old self.
