EYE.

World resides within the eye.

Process of Creation

I still remember the first brushstroke on this canvas. As the colors swirled and blended, an eye began to take shape, not just to see, but to take in the world, to notice the details that often go unnoticed. I spent hours blending the colors, lost in the process, and on that evenings, I looked up from my work and saw the most beautiful sunset from the studio.
